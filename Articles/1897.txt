Soyuz TMA-6 successfully launched
The Eneide Mission to the International Space Station carrying Italian ESA astronaut Roberto Vittori and two ISS Expedition 11 crewmembers made a perfect lift-off today from the Baikonur Cosmodrome, Kazakhstan, at 06:46 local time (02:46 CEST, 00:46 UT). More later.
