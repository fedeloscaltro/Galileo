YouTube SpaceLab winners watch their experiments live from space
Winners of the YouTube Spacelab competition saw their experiments performed in space on Thursday, 13 September. ESA astronaut Frank De Winne participated in the live event, transmitted from the International Space Station to the world via YouTube.
