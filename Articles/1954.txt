Life and Physical Sciences: new research opportunities
Through a recently released ESA Announcement of Opportunity, ESA's Directorate for Human Spaceflight invites scientists to propose new research projects in the areas of Life and Physical Sciences.
The projects can make use the following research platforms and facilities, available through ESA:
Potential investigators are requested to submit a non-binding Notice of Interest by 31 August 2004. Research proposals must be submitted by 12 November 2004.
To help scientists find their way though the proposal process, a workshop will be held on 13 September 2004 in the Erasmus User Centre at ESTEC in Noordwijk, The Netherlands.
For forms and more details see http://www.spaceflight.esa.int/users/lifephys
