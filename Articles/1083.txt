Follow the launch of ATV-3
Join us to follow the launch of ESA's third Automated Transfer Vehicle, Edoardo Amaldi, on Friday 23 March. ATV-3 will be launched on an Ariane 5 from Europe's Spaceport in Kourou, French Guiana, to the International Space Station. Launch is set for 05:34 CET (04:34 GMT). Streaming will start at approximately 05:00 CET (04:00 GMT).
