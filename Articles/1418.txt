ESA astronaut Frank De Winne arrives at International Space Station
The Soyuz TMA-15 spacecraft docked with the International Space Station at 14:34 CEST (12:34 CEST) today. The Soyuz crew, including ESA astronaut Frank De Winne, is scheduled to enter the ISS after hatch opening at 15:45 CEST (13:45 UT). Watch the replay of the docking.
