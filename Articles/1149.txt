Watch the replay of media event from ESTEC with André Kuipers
Watch the replay of live webcast from the PromISSe mission pre-launch media event that was held at ESA's Space Research and Technology Centre on Thursday 8 September 2011.
Programme included presention of the PromISSe mission and update on Soyuz status by ESA astronauts André Kuipers and Christer Fuglesang, and information about the Dutch involvement in ESA Programmes.
The presentations were held in English and Dutch.
The replay is available for downloading at ESA's online videos.
