ATV Johannes Kepler mission blog
ESA's unofficial but highly informative ATV Johannes Kepler blog is now live and will be updated regularly during count-down, launch, rendezvous and docking with the ISS - set for 26 February 2011.
The blog will include audio, video and pictures together with in-depth, behind-the-scene reports from Kourou, ATV Control Centre Toulouse and from ESA experts across the Agency. Access the blog via http://blogs.esa.int/atv.
