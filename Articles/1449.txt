Minigallery: Belgian royals visit European Astronaut Centre
Their Majesties Albert II King of the Belgians and Queen Paola visited the European Astronaut Centre (EAC) in Cologne, Germany, on 19 March 2009. At EAC they met ESA astronaut Frank De Winne who is currently training for his upcoming long-duration mission onboard the International Space Station.
