International Space Station
Heads of Agency speak with reporters
ESA Director General Jean-Jacques Dordain and his counterparts from the International Space Station partnership will speak with reporters today, 2 March, at 19:00 CET (13:00 EST) at the Kennedy Space Centre press site in Florida, USA.
Watch the conference at 19.00 CET
The news conference follows the International Space Station Heads of Agency meeting. The meeting brings together the leaders of the Canadian, European, Japanese, Russian, and U.S. space agencies. At this meeting, agency leaders will discuss plans for launching the remaining station components and operations during the assembly process.
In addition to Mr. Dordain, news conference participants include:
NASA Administrator Michael Griffin
Canadian Space Agency Acting President Virendra Jha
Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency President Keiji TachikawaRussian Federal Space Agency Head Anatolii Perminov
The news conference will air live on the NASA TV media and public channels.
