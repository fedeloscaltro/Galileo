Columbus launch targeted for 10 January
NASA has announced a new target date of 10 January for the launch of space shuttle Atlantis (STS-122), carrying ESA's Columbus laboratory to the International Space Station. The liftoff depends on resolution of the problem in a fuel sensor system.
