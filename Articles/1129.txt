Watch online - Mars500 press conference
Mars500 crew, including the two ESA volunteers Romain Charles from France and Diego Urbina from Italy, will meet the press today at 10:00 CET (13:00 local time in Moscow) at the RIA Novosti, Russian International News Agency. The webstreaming, provided by RIA Novosti, is available at www.ria.ru.
