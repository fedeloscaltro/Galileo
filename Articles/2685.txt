A space compass
A space compass is a multimedia lesson on rotational mechanics for upper secondary students (17-19 years old) focused on the rotational motion of rigid bodies and gyroscopic effects in a weightless environment. The lesson contains embedded video clips recorded on board the ISS and on ground.
