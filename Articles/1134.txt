Watch online - Mars500 hatch opening
At last the 'journey' is at an end for the Mars500 crew of six, who complete their simulated mission to Mars on 4 November. Join us to watch the opening of the hatch and the egress of the crew, including the two ESA volunteers Romain Charles from France and Diego Urbina from Italy. Streaming starts at 10:45 CET with hatch opening at 11.00 CET.
